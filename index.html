Meet Alexandra

Alexandra Brinzac is a being that journeys through life with an open heart and a deep love for all that is moving. Her background is a BA in psychology, with a master degree in experiential psychotherapy. During her last 4 years, her journey was paved with linking psychology and ecopsychology together. Therefore, you can find her hosting climate circles, deep ecology and The Work That Reconnects workshops and gathering people for forest bathing sessions.
